ABOUT ME

Hello, hello! I’m Henry — a Product Designer & Design Systems Specialist focused on helping designers and developers create together. I’ve worked for seven years in Design and for the last four I’ve led the Design System at Societe Generale while also working on an array of complex products and launching my own internal product. I’m a lover of painting, experimenting with food and more recently menswear.

WHAT I DO

I love working on complex tools and systems, while getting involved in all parts of design and devlopment — I'm happy getting stuck into anything! I have experience building a wide range of complex products and specific skills in all areas of running a Design System, from day to day component design to road-mapping and strategy. I have advanced Figma and front-end knowledge for working with Designers and Developers. I’m comfortable with complexity, project ownership and writing, editing and speaking. I also take on painting commissions in my free time!

SG DESIGN SYSTEM /
DESIGN SYSTEM LEAD
2019 — PRESENT

Over the past four years I’ve lead the delivery of SG’s Design System. This has included Figma libraries, web components and bespoke documentation used in over 800 products. I’ve worked on keeping the system alive with hundreds of updates, forming a successful “Design System Council” and I've overseen the adoption of a new business line and sale of the system usage to an external provider.

Operationally I have lead our small Design System council of four people and managed all road-maps and contributions from 40+ Designers and Developers. I am an expert in managing Design Systems in Figma and contribute significantly to the front-end builds of the web libraries.

SG DESIGN LAB /
PRODUCT OWNER & DESIGNER
2019 — PRESENT

I won an internal SG Digital Innovation Award in 2023 for my work on a tool called “Design Lab”, which began as a side project to help review Design -> Code. I negotiated budget to build the tool into an official product which has since grown to over 2000 regular users and 1100 editors, with a 7 minute average visit time. Use of the tool was also included in a deal with a client.

I expanded the feature set to become a main handover tool linking Figma libraries and bespoke documentation site. Designed the complete product but also built two major features which allow component libraries to be previewed and inserted into code.
