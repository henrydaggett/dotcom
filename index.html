ABOUT ME
Hello, hello! I’m Henry — a Product Designer & Design Systems Specialist focused on helping designers and developers create together. I’ve worked for seven years in Design and for the last four I’ve led the Design System at Societe Generale while also working on an array of complex products and launching my own internal product. I’m a lover of painting, experimenting with food and more recently menswear.
WHAT I DO
I love getting involved in all parts of design and devlopment - I'm happy getting stuck into anything! I have experience building a wide range of complex products and specific skills in all areas of running a Design System, from day to day component design to road-mapping and strategy. I have advanced Figma and front-end knowledge for working with Designers and Developers. I’m comfortable with complexity, project ownership and writing, editing and speaking. I also take on painting commissions in my free time!
